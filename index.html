Welcome to the Realm of Immortuae

This is a medieval modpack based in a dark but magical world where you can build powerful castles and kingdoms, or become a powerful rogue knight searching for a worthy opponent. This world is full of rival factions and ancient magic ready to be explored.

About the Modpack

This modpack is designed to enhance your Minecraft experience with a medieval theme, featuring various mods that add new gameplay elements, structures, and challenges. Whether you want to build grand castles, engage in epic battles, or explore mystical realms, this modpack has something for everyone.
