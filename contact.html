Thank you for your interest in the Realm of Immortuae modpack! We appreciate your support and look forward to hearing from you.

Q: How do I install the modpack?

A: You can download the modpack from our CurseForge page and follow the installation instructions provided there.

Q: Can I play with friends?

A: Yes! You can host a server or join our community server to play with others with your friends.

Q: What are the system requirements for the modpack?

A: The modpack requires a decent computer with at least 4GB of RAM allocated to Minecraft. We recommend at least 8GB of RAM for the best experience.

Q: How can I report a bug or issue?

A: You can report bugs or issues on our CurseForge page or by contacting us through Discord. Please provide as much detail as possible to help us resolve the issue quickly.

Q: Are there any rules for the community server?

A: Yes, we have a set of rules to ensure a friendly and enjoyable environment for all players. You can find the rules in our Discord server or on the community server itself at spawn.

Q: Where is the server IP Adress?

A: The server IP address is realmofimmortuae.kinetichosting.gg. You can join the server using this address in your Minecraft client with the modpack installed.
